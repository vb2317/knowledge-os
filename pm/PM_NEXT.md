# PM_NEXT.md — Product Strategy Backlog

Items to work through in future PM sessions.

---

## Immediate

- [ ] **Fill in Target User section** — Even if it's just you, write it out: context, constraints, habits, current workaround. Suggested starting point: "AI engineer, time-constrained, strong topic opinions, gets overwhelmed by volume, values depth over breadth."

- [ ] **Define Vision** — What does this look like when it's *done*, not shipped? One paragraph, not a feature list.

- [ ] **Define Goals (2–3 measurable outcomes)** — Not features. What changes for the user in 30 days? In 3 months?

- [ ] **Fill in Key Metrics** — What would you actually measure? Candidates: days/week digest is opened, links clicked per digest, engagement opp acted-on rate, days since last "switched off" moment.

---

## Strategy Questions to Resolve

- [ ] **Clarify implicit feedback signals** — "Gets better over time" is asserted but the mechanism is vague. What counts as implicit signal? Link clicks? Time to open? Ignoring a digest entirely? Decide before building the feedback loop.

- [ ] **Resolve the weekly unfiltered view tension** — Is it FOMO catch-up (show me what I missed) or awareness (show me what I chose not to see)? Different purposes lead to different designs. Pick one.

- [ ] **Non-goals** — Explicitly decide what this is *not*. e.g. Not a social feed. Not real-time. Not for others (yet). Writing this down prevents scope creep.

---

## Longer Term

- [ ] **Define "Phase A complete"** — Per the ChatGPT strategy doc: single-user experience should feel addictive in a good way. What's the checklist to declare Phase A done and move to 5-user alpha?

- [ ] **Taste Profiles / onboarding design** — If you ever open this to others, what's the minimum onboarding? Topic presets? A single "I care about X" prompt?
