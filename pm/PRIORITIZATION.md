# Prioritization

> PM coaching note: The most important PM skill is deciding what *not* to build. Every item you add is a cost — to focus, to complexity, to maintenance.

---

## RICE Framework

Score each item, then sort by RICE score. Build the highest-scoring things first.

| Factor | Question | Scale |
|--------|----------|-------|
| **Reach** | How many users/sessions does this affect per period? | # per week |
| **Impact** | How much does it move the needle per user? | 3=massive, 2=high, 1=medium, 0.5=low, 0.25=minimal |
| **Confidence** | How sure are you about Reach and Impact estimates? | 100%=high, 80%=medium, 50%=low |
| **Effort** | How many person-weeks? | person-weeks |

**RICE Score = (Reach × Impact × Confidence) / Effort**
