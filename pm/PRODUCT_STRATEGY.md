# Product Strategy

> PM coaching note: Fill this out before deciding what to build. "Why" and "who" come before "what."

Why: Amount of news and info in general has exploded, become insurmountable. 

Who: self - I am an AI engineer and I want to be aware of the latest goings-on. But opening any news site feels overwhelming these days. Show me some calm.

What: This product curates information based on personal prefrences, gets better over time (with explicit and implicit feedback from the user), shows weekly summary of unfiltered content, top trending topics that the user is not prefering. - FOMO + JOMO.
